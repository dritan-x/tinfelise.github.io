Introducing Dryerly

Get dry. Stay clean. Keep warm.

Think that damp towel on your bathroom floor is getting the job done? It's not.

  • Healthy. Damp towels collect mildew. Dry ones don't.
  • Better. Cleaner towels means you need to wash them less.
  • Simple. Turns on when you throw your towel in. Turns off when dry.
  • Efficient. Environmentally-friendly design uses less electicity.
